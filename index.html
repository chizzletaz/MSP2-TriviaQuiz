How to play the game:

Click on the ‘Start Playing’ button to choose in what game mode you want play. Challenge Mode or Practice Mode.

Challenge Mode:

In this mode there are three rounds to play. Each round the difficulty of the questions increases. Level 1 has easy questions, level 2 has medium questions and level 3 has hard questions.
The questions come from different kind of categories.
At the end of the game you can save your score and see if you made it to the high scores or you can play again.

Practice Mode:

In this mode you can select the category you want the questions to be from and the difficulty level. This mode has only 1 round.

When you're playing the game, you can control the music and sound settings. Just click on the icon to toggle between on and off.
